Construction Safety Project

Participant Survey Procedure

To ensure equal priority and a balanced distribution of tasks among the participants, the 150 participants have been divided into three groups. Each group will complete the tasks in a different sequence, focusing on either reading, video, or AR-based training first. Below is a breakdown of the procedure for each group:

Group 1: Reading First

1. Start with the Reading Manual: Participants begin by reading the construction safety manual.

2. Fill out the Reading Survey: After completing the reading, participants will complete the Reading Survey.

3. Start the Video Manual: Next, participants will watch the safety video manual.

4. Fill out the Video Survey: Upon finishing the video, participants will complete the Video Survey.

5. Start the AR Module: Finally, participants will engage with the Augmented Reality (AR) module.

6. Fill out the AR Survey: After completing the AR module, participants will fill out the AR Survey.

Group 2: Video First

1. Start with the Video Manual: Participants begin by watching the safety video manual.

2. Fill out the Video Survey: After completing the video, participants will complete the Video Survey.

3. Start the Reading Manual: Next, participants will read the construction safety manual.

4. Fill out the Reading Survey: After reading the manual, participants will fill out the Reading Survey.

5. Start the AR Module: Finally, participants will engage with the Augmented Reality (AR) module.

6. Fill out the AR Survey: After completing the AR module, participants will fill out the AR Survey.

Group 3: AR First

1. Start with the AR Module: Participants begin by engaging with the Augmented Reality (AR) module.

2. Fill out the AR Survey: After completing the AR module, participants will fill out the AR Survey.

3. Start the Video Manual: Next, participants will watch the safety video manual.

4. Fill out the Video Survey: Upon finishing the video, participants will complete the Video Survey.

5. Start the Reading Manual: Finally, participants will read the construction safety manual.

6. Fill out the Reading Survey: After completing the reading, participants will fill out the Reading Survey.
